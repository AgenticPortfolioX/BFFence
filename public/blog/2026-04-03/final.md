# Spring Fence Preparation in Michigan

As the snow melts and the ground thaws, it's the perfect time to evaluate your property's perimeter. Michigan winters can be harsh on outdoor structures, making spring the ideal season for maintenance or new installations.

## Why Western Red Cedar?

At BF Fence, we exclusively recommend **Western Red Cedar** for our board-on-board and privacy fences. Here's why:

1. **Natural Resistance**: Cedar contains natural oils that resist moisture, decay, and insect damage.
2. **Stability**: Unlike pressure-treated pine, cedar is less likely to warp or shrink as it dries.
3. **Aesthetic**: The rich, warm tones of cedar provide an immediately premium look to any backyard.

## Getting Ready for Installation

If you're planning a new fence this season, ensure your property lines are marked and any old structures are cleared. Beat the summer rush by booking your estimate in April!
